The Best Movies According to Praise

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Fast and Furious

Fast & Furious, also known as The Fast and the Furious, is a media franchise centered on a series of action films
that are largely concerned with street racing, heists, spies, and family.
The franchise also includes short films, a television series, toys, video games, live shows, and theme park attractions.

Gladiator

Set in Roman times, the story of a once-powerful general forced to become a common gladiator.
The emperor's son is enraged when he is passed over as heir in favour of his father's favourite general.
He kills his father and arranges the murder of the general's family, and the general is sold into slavery to be trained as a gladiator
- but his subsequent popularity in the arena threatens the throne

Alexander

- Age: Born on 20/21 July 356 BC and died on 10/11 June 323 BC
- Reign: King of Macedon at 20, succeeded his father Philip II
- Military campaigns: Conducted a lengthy campaign throughout Western Asia, Central Asia, parts of South Asia, and Egypt
- Empire: By 30, he had created one of the largest empires in history, stretching from Greece to northwestern India
- Battlefield: Undefeated in battle and considered one of history's greatest military commanders
- Education: Tutored by Aristotle until the age of 16
- Leadership: Led the League of Corinth and launched the pan-Hellenic project against Persia
- Personality: Charismatic, brilliant, ruthless, bloodthirsty, and power-hungry
- Influence: Spread Greek culture, language, and thought throughout his empire and initiated the Hellenistic Period
- Death: Died in 323 BC without naming a successor, dividing his empire among his generals
